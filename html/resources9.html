Learn how to make informed decisions about healthcare and insurance options

21 December 2023, 10:30am


Factors to consider when choosing the best healthcare and insurance plan for elderlies:

  • Your age, health condition, and medical needs
  • Your income, savings, and assets
  • Your eligibility for government subsidies and benefits
  • Your preferences and lifestyle

Next step to choose a healthcare and insurance plan:

  • Compare the different healthcare and insurance options that are available to you,
  • see which one best meets your needs and budget
  • Use the Medicare Plan Finder to compare the costs, benefits, and ratings of different plans
  • Consult with a licensed insurance agent, a financial planner,
  • or a trusted family member or friend to help you with your decision
