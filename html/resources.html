We create Money Maniacs to serve as an educational platform, imparting essential financial concepts like inflation, practical money-saving strategies, and various investment options. The application,
equipped with real-time inflation rate data through an API, aims to enhance users' financial literacy and improve their money management skills. Additionally, Money Maniacs features a budgeting
tutorial game that empowers users to track and manage their expenses effectively throughout the week.

Financial literacy refers to the ability to understand and apply financial knowledge, enabling individuals to make informed and responsible decisions about their money.
It involves skills such as budgeting, investing, managing debt, and comprehending economic concepts, ultimately empowering individuals to achieve financial well-being.
