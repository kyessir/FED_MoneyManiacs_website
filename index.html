FAQ

We create Money Maniacs to serve as an educational platform, imparting essential financial concepts like inflation, practical money-saving strategies, and various investment options. The application, equipped with real-time inflation rate data through an API, aims to enhance users' financial literacy and improve their money management skills. Additionally, Money Maniacs features a budgeting tutorial game that empowers users to track and manage their expenses effectively throughout the week.

Financial literacy refers to the ability to understand and apply financial knowledge, enabling individuals to make informed and responsible decisions about their money. It involves skills such as budgeting, investing, managing debt, and comprehending economic concepts, ultimately empowering individuals to achieve financial well-being.

We're considering launching a series of webinars and workshops led by financial experts to provide additional learning opportunities for our users.

We employ a multifaceted approach to staying updated with the ever-evolving landscape of the financial industry. This includes regular monitoring of reputable financial news sources, attending industry conferences and seminars, maintaining active memberships in professional organizations, and fostering partnerships with experts and institutions within the field. Additionally, our team of financial analysts and educators continuously research and analyze market trends, regulatory changes, and emerging technologies to ensure our educational content remains current, insightful, and relevant for our users. By staying abreast of the latest developments, we can adapt our materials to provide users with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed financial decisions in today's dynamic environment.
